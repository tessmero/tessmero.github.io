Tailored simulations are designed for one specific application. As a rule of thumb, they are developed by starting with a more standard [procedural](/techs/procedural-motion) or [dynamic](/techs/dynamic-sim) strategy. They can have special properties like being deterministic ([paintball](/paintball)) or handling large numbers of particles ([sand](/sand))
